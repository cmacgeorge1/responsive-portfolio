About me

Hello everyone! My name is Chris. Welcome to my page! Let me tell you a little about myself....

I was born and raised in the Greater Seattle Area. After high school I attended Eastern Washington University where I studied international relations and military science, as well as served in the Washington Army National Guard.

After graduation, I was commissioned as an infantry officer in the United States Army. I was stationed at Fort Benning, GA, then Fort Campbell, KY with the 101st Airborne Division. Shortly after arriving, my unit deployed to Eastern Afghanistan as part of the forces surge in 2010. While there, I served as a platoon leader and outpost commander.

After leaving the Army in 2014, I began working in private security program and project management. Now I am exploring a new field of coding and web development (which turns out to be useful for building a photography portfolio online).

I enjoy spending time outdoors with my dog, traveling (pre COVID), and my primary hobby, photography (click on Portfolio in the navbar to check out a few shots).
